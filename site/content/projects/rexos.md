RexOS (Retro Experience OS) is a custom operating system for retro gaming handhelds like Anbernic devices.

## Goals

- **Fast boot** — Get to games quickly
- **Clean UI** — No clutter, just your library
- **Reliable** — No crashes mid-session
- **Hackable** — Easy to customize and extend

## Architecture

Written primarily in Rust with some C for hardware-specific drivers. Targets ARM-based retro gaming handhelds.

Currently in active development.
