## The short version

I build infrastructure and data platforms. Backend systems, automation, dev tools, CLI stuff. If it runs in a terminal or orchestrates something, I'm probably interested.

Day job involves clouds, containers, pipelines, and making systems that don't wake people up at 3am. Been doing this across banks, AI companies, and startups of various sizes.

## NeuroBits

This is **NeuroBits**. Personal space for notes, experiments, and projects. The name is about small bits of knowledge wiring together over time.

**Blog** for longer posts. **Notes** for quick captures. **Projects** for things I'm building.

No analytics. No tracking. Just static files.

## Philosophy

> *Make the right thing the easy thing.*

Understand the problem first. Build the simplest thing that works. Delete more than you write. Automate the boring parts.
