Send Files and Folders With Netcat

Written by Rubens Santos - z1ron with ♥ on August 30, 2019 in tips

server:

$ mkdir ${destination} && cd ${destination} # your custom folder path
$ nc -l ${port} | tar xf -

client:

$ cd ${dir} # your dir to send
$ tar cf - . | nc ${server_ip} ${port}
